Meet the Owners

Zee Winegart, Owner

Amanda Winegart, Co-Owner

Welcome to ZEE'S SMASH, the go-to spot in Tulare, CA, for mouthwatering burgers, Mediterranean delights, and more! We pride ourselves on serving up delicious, handcrafted meals that are perfect for any occasion. Our burgers are made with the freshest ingredients, grilled to perfection, and packed with flavor in every bite. In addition to our fantastic burgers, we also offer a range of authentic Mediterranean dishes, from savory gyros to flavorful kebabs, providing a diverse menu that caters to all tastes.

At ZEE'S SMASH, we believe in quality and taste. Our friendly team is dedicated to providing a welcoming and relaxed dining experience for our guests. Whether you're craving a classic burger, a Mediterranean platter, or a tasty side, we've got something to satisfy every appetite. Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or a meal with friends, you'll find a warm and inviting atmosphere here. Come join us and discover why ZEE'S SMASH is the ultimate destination for both burger and Mediterranean lovers in Tulare!
